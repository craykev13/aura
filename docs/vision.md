# AURA Vision

## What is AURA?

AURA is an AI Chief of Staff designed to help knowledge workers manage information, automate repetitive work, and coordinate intelligent agents through a single, unified workspace.

Unlike traditional AI chatbots that simply answer questions, AURA is designed to understand context, proactively organize work, and assist users throughout their day while always keeping the user in control.

The long-term vision is to create software that feels less like a chatbot and more like an intelligent operating system.

---

## Mission

Build an AI workspace that helps people spend less time managing work and more time doing meaningful work.

---

## Goals

* Organize information automatically.
* Reduce repetitive tasks.
* Coordinate multiple AI agents.
* Keep humans in control.
* Be beautiful to use.
* Be fast.
* Be trustworthy.
* Be highly customizable.

---

## What AURA Is NOT

AURA is not trying to replace the user.

AURA is not trying to become AGI.

AURA is not designed to make decisions without user oversight.

AURA exists to assist, organize, automate, and simplify.

---

## Long-Term Vision

AURA should eventually become a user's primary digital workspace.

Instead of opening multiple applications throughout the day, users should be able to manage their work through one intelligent interface.
